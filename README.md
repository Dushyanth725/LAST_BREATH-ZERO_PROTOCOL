# LAST BREATH – ZERO PROTOCOL

> *"When survival becomes the only protocol left."*

## About the Game

**Last Breath – Zero Protocol** is a story-driven, single-player narrative game set inside a nuclear power plant where a routine workday suddenly turns into a catastrophic disaster.

The game follows **Mark**, an ordinary employee working overtime with his close friend **Chris**. What begins as another exhausting shift quickly escalates into a reactor accident that traps workers inside the facility.

As the player, you must navigate dangerous laboratory sectors, solve environmental challenges, make difficult decisions, and uncover the truth behind the incident while fighting to stay alive.

## Story Synopsis

After receiving a mandatory overtime notice, Mark and Chris prepare to leave work after another long shift. Moments later, a violent explosion shakes the nuclear facility.

Emergency alarms echo through the plant.

Communication is lost.

Power systems begin to fail.

With no idea what caused the accident, the two workers enter the collapsing laboratory in search of answers—and a way out.

But the deeper they go, the more they realize the disaster wasn't entirely accidental.

## Main Characters

### Mark (Player)

An ordinary nuclear plant worker forced into an extraordinary situation.

Calm under pressure but emotionally driven, Mark wants nothing more than to survive and protect those around him.

---

### Chris

Mark's closest friend and coworker.

A hardworking family man struggling to balance work and personal life. Chris serves as the player's companion during the early sections of the game.

---

### The Antagonist

A mysterious individual connected to the reactor incident.

Rather than being purely evil, the antagonist challenges Mark's beliefs and reveals the hidden truth behind the accident, forcing the player to question who is truly responsible.

---

## Gameplay

Players progress through the story by:

- Exploring different sectors of the facility
- Following NPC instructions naturally through dialogue
- Solving puzzles
- Restoring critical systems
- Escaping hazardous environments
- Making narrative-driven decisions
- Discovering hidden documents and environmental clues

Objectives are introduced organically through conversations rather than traditional tutorial pop-ups.

## Narrative Structure

### Prologue
Introduces the characters, their everyday struggles, and the overtime shift before the reactor incident.

### Main Story
Players explore the damaged facility while uncovering the truth behind the catastrophe.

### Confrontation
Mark faces the antagonist, who reveals a different perspective on the accident.

### Epilogue
The aftermath of the incident is presented through a media interview, allowing the story to conclude on an emotional note.

---

## Development Philosophy

Our goal is to create a narrative experience where gameplay and storytelling work together.

Instead of interrupting gameplay with tutorials, NPC dialogue naturally guides players through objectives while maintaining immersion.

Every conversation is written to feel believable and emotionally grounded, helping players connect with the characters throughout the journey.
